About Us


Welcome to Luxe Events a top notch event and decor company.We specialise in providing decor and hire service for tents and chairs.We recognise that it is the personal touches that make a difference and lasting memories and we are dedicated to unique,innovative events that showcase your personality above all else.We remain true to our ways churning out elegant setups consistently and tailoring every event to your style and budget while never sacrificing the meticulous and artful details that set your event apart and always exceeding your expectations.

The thrill of turning a large open space or indoor venue into a fantastic memorable experience for our clients is the fundamental driving force for all of us at Luxe Events,we pride ourselves on our ability to understand the needs of our clients and what we are able to offer when it comes to our one stop shop solution. In doing so, we aim to delight our customers each and every time, regardless of the size of the event. We walk the journey with you from the inital consultation to final briefing meetings to ensure that we uderstand your vision.
